## Company blurb

SEO Inc. is a digital marketing agency and digital marketing company specializing in SEO services, PPC management, AI search optimization, Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), LLM visibility, and technical SEO. The agency helps companies improve visibility across Google, paid search, AI-powered discovery platforms, and conversion-focused digital channels.

## Company size

Medium team, 51-200 employees.

## Remote status

SEO Inc. is remote-friendly for eligible roles in SEO, PPC, analytics, content strategy, AI search optimization, technical SEO, development, and digital marketing operations. Some roles may require U.S. business-hour availability, client communication, performance reporting, and collaboration with leadership.

## Region

SEO Inc. primarily hires in the Americas, with a focus on U.S.-based candidates.

## Company technologies

SEO Inc. works across WordPress, JavaScript, PHP, Cloudflare, CDN optimization, AWS S3, analytics platforms, structured data, Schema Markup, Core Web Vitals, page speed optimization, conversion tracking, Google Search Console, Google Ads, GA4, tag management, SEO automation, AI tools, LLM visibility workflows, and data-driven search marketing systems.

The team focuses on technical SEO, content optimization, E-E-A-T alignment, crawlability, indexation, structured data, site performance, paid search efficiency, and measurable lead-generation outcomes.

## Office locations

Nashville, Tennessee and Carlsbad, California.
