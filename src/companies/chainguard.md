## Company blurb

[Chainguard](https://www.chainguard.dev/) is a software supply chain security company best known for [Chainguard Images](https://www.chainguard.dev/chainguard-images) — minimal, hardened, zero-known-CVE container images built from source. The company also produces SBOM and attestation tooling, and contributes heavily to open-source projects in the supply chain space, including [Sigstore](https://www.sigstore.dev/) and [Wolfi](https://wolfi.dev/).

Chainguard was founded by engineers from Google's Open Source Security Team and has become a key vendor for organizations adopting SLSA-style supply chain controls.

## Company size

Approximately 200-300 employees.

## Remote status

Chainguard is a fully remote company. The team is distributed across the U.S., Ireland, and other countries, with bi-annual destination offsites bringing the company together in person.

## Region

Worldwide, with a strong U.S. and EMEA presence.

## Company technologies

- Go (the primary language across the platform)
- Wolfi (their undistro Linux base)
- Sigstore for signing and verification
- Kubernetes and major cloud providers
- Open-source-first — substantial contributions to OSS supply chain tooling
