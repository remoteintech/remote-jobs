## Company blurb

[PostHog](https://posthog.com/) is an open-source product analytics platform for engineers. It bundles product analytics, session replay, feature flags, A/B testing, surveys, and a data warehouse into one self-hostable or cloud-hosted product.

PostHog is open-core (MIT-licensed for the core platform) and operates publicly: company strategy, salary bands, and team-level handbooks are all published on the company website.

## Company size

Approximately 80-100 employees.

## Remote status

PostHog is a remote-first company. Small teams operate autonomously, communication defaults to async (and public, in their public Slack and handbook), and roles are open to candidates within timezone bands roughly GMT-8 to GMT+2.

## Region

Worldwide, with a North America and Europe time-zone preference.

## Company technologies

- Python (Django) for the backend
- ClickHouse for analytics queries
- TypeScript and React for the frontend
- Kafka and Redis for ingestion and queueing
- Self-hostable on Kubernetes; managed cloud on AWS
