## Company blurb

[Render](https://render.com/) is a unified cloud platform for hosting web services, static sites, background workers, cron jobs, and managed databases. It is frequently described as a modern Heroku alternative, with first-class support for Docker, autoscaling, private networking, and zero-downtime deploys.

Render targets teams that want managed infrastructure without managing Kubernetes themselves.

## Company size

Approximately 100-150 employees.

## Remote status

Render is a remote-first company with a distributed team across the United States and Canada, plus an optional San Francisco office. The company offers a remote work stipend and a fully equipped home-office benefit.

## Region

Americas. Most roles are open across U.S. and Canadian time zones.

## Company technologies

- Go for the platform's control and data planes
- TypeScript and React for the dashboard
- Kubernetes under the hood
- Postgres for managed databases
- AWS as the underlying cloud
