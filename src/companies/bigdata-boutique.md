## Company blurb

[BigData Boutique](https://bigdataboutique.com) is a leading big data consultancy specializing in designing, building, and optimizing scalable data platforms using open-source technologies. We work with Fortune 100 enterprises and innovative startups alike, providing hands-on consulting, managed services, and 24/7 support for technologies like Elasticsearch, OpenSearch, Apache Kafka, Apache Spark, ClickHouse, and more.

BigData Boutique is a globally distributed team. Most engineering and support roles are offered as fully remote positions, available from anywhere in the world. Our distributed nature enables true 24/7 support coverage across time zones. Some roles are based in Israel with hybrid arrangements.
