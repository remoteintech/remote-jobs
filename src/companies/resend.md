## Company blurb

[Resend](https://resend.com/) is a developer-focused email platform offering a transactional and marketing email API, batch sending, scheduled delivery, webhooks, and high-quality deliverability. The company also maintains [React Email](https://react.email/), an open-source library for building email templates with React components.

Resend is positioned as a modern alternative to legacy email APIs, with a strong focus on the developer experience and design of both the dashboard and the SDKs.
