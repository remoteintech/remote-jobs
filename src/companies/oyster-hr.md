## Company blurb

[Oyster HR](https://www.oysterhr.com/) is a global employment platform that lets companies hire, pay, and manage team members in more than 180 countries. Oyster operates as an Employer of Record (EOR), handling local employment contracts, payroll, taxes, benefits, and compliance for distributed teams.

The company positions itself as a mission-driven business focused on enabling fair, location-agnostic hiring at global scale.

## Company size

Approximately 600+ employees.

## Remote status

Oyster is a fully remote company — "work from anywhere — we mean it." The team is spread across more than 70 countries, and the product itself is built specifically for distributed companies.

## Region

Worldwide.

## Company technologies

- TypeScript and Node.js for the platform
- React for the web application
- GraphQL APIs
- Postgres
- AWS for infrastructure
