## Company blurb

[Tailscale](https://tailscale.com/) builds a zero-config mesh VPN on top of [WireGuard](https://www.wireguard.com/), making it simple to connect devices, servers, and cloud resources across networks without exposing them to the public internet. The product is widely used for secure remote access, site-to-site connectivity, and replacing traditional corporate VPNs.

Tailscale is free for personal use and offers paid plans for teams and enterprises, with a strong focus on identity-aware networking.

## Company size

Approximately 200-300 employees.

## Remote status

Tailscale is a remote-first company — "most of us work wherever there's wifi." The team is distributed across Canada, the United States, and the United Kingdom, with periodic in-person team meetups.

## Region

Americas and Europe. Most roles are open across Canada, the U.S., and the U.K.

## Company technologies

- Go for nearly the entire stack
- WireGuard as the underlying protocol
- TypeScript and React for the admin console
- Cloud-hosted control plane
- Native clients across macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android
