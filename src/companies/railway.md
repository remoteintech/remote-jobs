## Company blurb

[Railway](https://railway.com/) is an infrastructure platform that lets developers deploy applications, databases, and background workers without configuring DevOps. It supports Dockerfiles, Nixpacks-based builds, preview environments, and a wide library of one-click templates.

Railway is widely used by startups and indie developers for everything from side projects to production workloads, and the company has a strong open-source community presence.

## Company size

Approximately 30-50 employees.

## Remote status

Railway is a fully remote company. The team is described as "autonomous, async-heavy, and hires globally," and all listed positions are open from anywhere.

## Region

Worldwide. Railway hires across more than 17 countries.

## Company technologies

- Go for backend services and the build pipeline
- TypeScript and React for the dashboard
- Kubernetes for the underlying infrastructure
- Postgres and Redis
- Multi-cloud presence
