              ## Company blurb

              Lorum is a regulated specialist correspondent institution providing multi-currency clearing, custody accounts, and cash management for financial platforms globally. The company serves payroll and EOR platforms, fintech and payment service providers, trading and investment platforms, and marketplaces. Lorum's Named Account Custody model provides direct contractual and KYC links per end customer, removing the compliance gaps and de-banking risks common in nested correspondent banking.

              ## Company size

              50-100 employees

              ## Remote status

              Remote-friendly with offices across multiple continents. Engineers have flexibility in where and how they work, with collaboration happening across distributed teams. The engineering team works closely with Product, Operations, Security, and GRC functions, so clear async communication matters. No strict timezone mandate, but overlap with colleagues is expected for collaborative work.

              ## Region

              Worldwide. Lorum hires across Europe, the Middle East, the United States, and Asia.
