## Company blurb

[Cal.com](https://cal.com/) is an open-source scheduling infrastructure platform — an alternative to Calendly that can be self-hosted, white-labeled, or embedded into other products via Cal Atoms. Beyond individual scheduling, Cal.com offers team and enterprise features, routing forms, and a managed cloud platform.

The core product is licensed under AGPLv3 and developed publicly on [GitHub](https://github.com/calcom/cal.com).

## Company size

Approximately 50-80 employees.

## Remote status

Cal.com is a fully distributed company. Per its about page, the team works entirely online, with no physical headquarters and no plans for one.

## Region

Worldwide. Cal.com hires across the Americas, Europe, and Asia-Pacific.

## Company technologies

- TypeScript and Next.js
- React for the web client and embeddable widgets
- Prisma and PostgreSQL
- tRPC for type-safe APIs
- Vercel and AWS for hosting
