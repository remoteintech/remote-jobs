## Company blurb

PhantomBuster is a cloud-based automation platform that enables sales and marketing teams to extract data and automate actions across the web — no coding required. Their library of "Phantoms" covers workflows for LinkedIn, Sales Navigator, and dozens of other platforms, helping teams build lead lists, enrich CRM data, and run outreach at scale.

PhantomBuster is a fully remote company with team members distributed across multiple countries.
